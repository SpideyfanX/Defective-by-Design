# Defective-by-Design

Defective by Design is a third-person survival game where you play as an android that is marked as defective, desperately evading a growing squad of sleek, newer units sent to bring him offline. Each level drops you into a larger, more complex environment with more pursuers closing in, with a larger timer — what starts as a tense one-on-one chase quickly escalates into a desperate scramble through sprawling facilities, open yards, and tight corridors as the odds stack against you. This initial prototype is an example level with a 60-second timer and a single enemy for testing the main mechanics, along with the pause menu and victory and defeat mechanics.

Survive until time runs out, and you move on to the next level. But every level that passes means more hunters, more ground to cover, and less room for error, and longer time to try and survive. There are no weapons. No allies. No shortcuts. Just you, the clock, and however long your legs hold out.
